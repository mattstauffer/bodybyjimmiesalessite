About Jimmie Jennings

Born and raised in Ocala, Florida, Jimmie Jennings has become a fitness icon in Gainesville with his Body by Jimmie Fitness Program. From pageant girls to athletes to everyday housewives he has created workout programs that produce results. Whether you have a yearly gym membership or a free space in the garage, these workouts will get you into shape!

In signing up with Jimmie Jennings you become part of the Jimmie FAMILY and you get treated as such.

Taking 30 minutes to an hour each day to do something good for yourself is key in gaining the results you strive for. Upon signing up you are making the decision to dedicate yourself to an active and healthy lifestyle. It means that with willpower, determination and a whole lot of sweat, you are going to travel on a fitness journey and achieve real results and become the best version of yourself possible. Becoming a part of the Jimmie family also means that you will have access to Jimmie and Heather at all times whether you need encouragement, motivation, clarification on a workout move or a new recipe to throw in your cook book.

Fitness is what you make it, you get out as much as you are willing to put in. There is no better time than NOW to start changing your life.

It's time to turn impossible into I AM POSSIBLE. It's time to TRAIN HARDER.
